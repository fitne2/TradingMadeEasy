Investing Basics

What is Investing?

Investing is the process of allocating resources, usually money, with the expectation of generating income or capital appreciation over time. Investing can involve purchasing assets such as stocks, bonds, real estate, or other types of investments.

Budgeting and Saving

Budgeting is an essential tool for managing your money and reaching your financial goals. It involves tracking your income and expenses, setting financial goals, and making a plan to allocate your resources accordingly. Saving is setting aside a portion of your income to achieve short-term or long-term financial goals.

Major Financial Markets

Financial markets are places where financial assets such as stocks, bonds, and commodities are traded. They play a crucial role in allocating resources in the economy and influencing the returns on investments. Some of the major financial markets include stock markets, bond markets, and commodity markets.
